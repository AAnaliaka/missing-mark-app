# MMUST Missing Marks Management System

MissingMarks APP is a dedicated university tracking system designed to bridge the gap between students and lecturers when academic marks go missing. The platform transforms the traditionally slow, manual process of reporting grade discrepancies into a structured digital workflow, ensuring transparency and accountability for both parties.

## For Students: Track Your Academic Success

The student portal is designed to eliminate the anxiety of missing grades by providing clear visibility into the resolution process.

*   **Instant Reporting**: Students can file a missing mark report for any registered unit directly from their dashboard. The system captures all necessary details including course codes, semesters, and student descriptions to ensure the lecturer has everything they need to begin an investigation.
*   **Live Resolution Progress**: The home dashboard features a real-time resolution bar. This automatically calculates the percentage of your academic record that is complete versus pending, giving you a clear overview of your current standing.
*   **Interactive Progress Timeline**: Every report features a dedicated timeline. You can see exactly when a lecturer received your request, when they started the investigation, and the final outcome. This removes the need for frequent office visits or follow-up emails.
*   **Real-Time Status Notifications**: The app sends instant alerts to your phone's status bar the moment a lecturer updates your case. Whether your mark has been resolved or more information is required, you stay informed without needing to manually refresh the app.

## For Lecturers: Centralized Request Management

The lecturer portal provides a professional environment to manage and resolve student discrepancies efficiently.

*   **Unified Dashboard**: Lecturers see every pending request from every student in one organized list, categorized by status (Submitted, Investigating, Resolved, or Rejected).
*   **Structured Lifecycle**: The system guides lecturers through a professional workflow. One-tap actions allow you to move requests through the investigation stages, keeping the student's record updated automatically.
*   **Direct Communication & Rejection Logic**: If a request cannot be fulfilled, lecturers can provide specific rejection reasons. These are communicated instantly to the student, ensuring they understand the next steps or missing requirements.
*   **Instant Mark Updates**: Once a discrepancy is resolved, the lecturer can update the grade within the app. This change is reflected instantly on the student’s portal and academic profile.

## Key System Capabilities

*   **Real-Time Cloud Synchronization**: Built on a cloud-first architecture, every change made by a lecturer—from status updates to mark corrections—is visible to the student within seconds.
*   **Smart Academic Profiles**: The system automatically generates student profiles including School, Department, and Year of Study, ensuring that all reports are correctly routed and recorded.
*   **Notification History**: A dedicated history tab keeps a permanent record of all alerts and status changes, providing an audit trail for the entire semester.
*   **Secure Access Control**: The platform uses role-based security to ensure that students only access their personal data, while lecturers can manage their entire unit queue securely.

## Getting Started and Testing

### To test as a Lecturer:
1.  Sign up with any email address.
2.  On the profile completion screen, enter **ADMIN_LECTURER** as your Registration Number.
3.  The system will instantly upgrade your account to the Lecturer Dashboard.

### To test as a Student:
1.  Sign up with your university email.
2.  Complete your academic profile details.
3.  The system will automatically generate a set of sample units for you to begin testing the reporting and resolution workflow.
